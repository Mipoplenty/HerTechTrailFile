Bop Bop Ricard

The Resturant

The iconic Bob Bob Ricard Soho, located just off Regent Street, serves classic British & French menu in London'smost glamorous all-booth dining room. Equipped with a “Press for Champagne” button at every table, it is famous for pouring more champagne than any other restaurant in Britain.

Reservations

Reservations are accepted for full meals only. The largest booth size is 8 guests. Parties of 9 to 16 guests are accommodated in the private dining room.
